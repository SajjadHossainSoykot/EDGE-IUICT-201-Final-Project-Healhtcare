Health Tips

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to keep your body hydrated. Aim for at least 8 glass/day.

Balanced Diet

Fruits, veggies, lean proteins. Limit processed foods, sugar, and fats

Regular Exercise

Aim for 30 mins of moderate activity most days, like walking, cycling etc.

Get Enough Sleep

Aim for 7-9 hours per night for proper recovery and function.
