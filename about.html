About Soykot Healthcare

Soykot Healthcare, founded by Mr. Sajjad Hossain Soykot, is committed to providing compassionate and comprehensive healthcare services to our community. With a focus on excellence and innovation, we strive to deliver the highest quality of care to our patients, empowering them to lead healthier lives.

Mr. Sajjad Hossain Soykot

Mr. Sajjad Hossain Soykot is a renowned healthcare entrepreneur with a vision to revolutionize the healthcare industry. He holds a Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) degree from a prestigious medical university and has extensive experience in healthcare management and administration. As the founder of Soykot Healthcare, he is dedicated to improving healthcare accessibility and affordability for all.
